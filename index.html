In a bold fusion of traditional craftsmanship and technology, Coterie proudly launches its latest garment; a groundbreaking piece of clothing that seamlessly blends the intricate artistry of circuit design with Hiut Denim. Created by a talented team of female designers, each bringing their own unique perspective and expertise to the table. This fresh innovative garment – featuring a circuit-inspired aesthetic – was created out of a shared interest to explore the potential of creating a piece of clothing that can be worn in multiple ways across everyday life. Although this garment takes inspiration from technological advancements, the overall creation has remained sustainable and true to Hiut’s roots as a denim brand, by creating a high-quality, sustainable and durable denim garment.

Hiut, which is known for its Welsh heritage, craftsmanship and sustainability, is playing a key role in this collaboration, providing premium denim that serves as the foundation of the design. Hiut is a brand that puts an emphasis on quality and long-lasting garments, making for a perfect collaboration with Coterie. Coterie is a forward-thinking and female lead brand dedicated to pushing the boundaries of design through innovation and sustainability. Coterie has a strong commitment to creating pieces that celebrate individuality, creativity and comfort.
